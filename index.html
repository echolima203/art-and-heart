Discover Béziers

Saint-Nazaire Cathedral — a 4-minute walk away

One of France's oldest cities — wine culture, stunning architecture and the UNESCO-listed Canal du Midi. Mediterranean beaches, vineyards and rich gastronomy, without the crowds.

Saint-Nazaire Cathedral 4 min walk

The hilltop landmark with sweeping views over the Orb valley — free entry and a climbable tower.

The 9 Locks of Fonseranes Canal du Midi

A spectacular "water staircase" on the UNESCO canal — one of the region's most-visited sights.

~3 km away

Les Halles — covered market Local life

The restored 1891 cast-iron market hall — fresh produce, oysters and a glass of local wine.

~5 min walk

Pont Vieux & the riverside Photo spot

The 12th-century "Old Bridge" frames the postcard view of the cathedral above the town.

🎉 Feria de Béziers — every August the city hosts its famous five-day festival: street parties, flamenco and music, drawing over a million visitors.
